About Me

Currently, I live in North Carolina.
When not writing, I enjoy spending time with my family, including our two cats.

Why I Write

I started, and continue, to write because I want to bring stories that show everyone and anyone is worthy of love,
I hope that those who read my stories will be able to see that the world is a beautiful and kind place.
and that each and everyone of you deserves a life full of love and kindess.
