About Me

Hello, and welcome everyone!
I am an aspiring writer and an avid reader, currently living in North Carolina.
Currently, I have written and published several short stories in anthologies, but I am currently working on two novels.
When not writing, I enjoy spending time with my family; cat, child, and husband.
I of course love books, especially the young adult and romance genres. I love, and am inspired by,
the works of Maggie Steifvater, Tahereh Mari, and Casey McQuiston.
When I have free time, I also enjoy playing games, especially Story of Seasons and Dragon Age.


Why I Write

I started, and continue, to write because I want to bring stories that show everyone and anyone is worthy,
and deserves, a life of love and kindness, no matter what.
I hope that those who read my stories will be able to see that the world is a beautiful and kind place,
full of interesting and wonderful people.
